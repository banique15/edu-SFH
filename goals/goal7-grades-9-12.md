## Objectives

### Knowledge
- Understand the fundamental meaning and global importance of Global Goal 7: Affordable and Clean Energy, recognizing its crucial role in sustainable development. (NCCAS: Cr1.1.la, Cn10.1.la)
- Identify the key ideas of Global Goal 7, including universal energy access, increasing renewable energy share, doubling energy efforts, enhancing international cooperation, and expanding sustainable infrastructure. (NCCAS: Re7.1.la)
- Learn about the complex challenges and diverse potential solutions related to achieving affordable and clean energy access, promoting renewable energy sources, improving energy efficiency, fostering international cooperation, and developing sustainable infrastructure globally. (NCCAS: Cn10.1.la, Cn11.1.la)
- Explore the critical connections between energy production and consumption, environmental sustainability, economic development, and overall human well-being, recognizing the far-reaching impacts of our energy choices. (NCCAS: Cn11.1.la)

### Discourse
- Express their developing understanding of abstract energy concepts and related global challenges through the tangible mediums of art, music, creative writing, drama, and dance. (NCCAS: Cr2.1.la, Pr5.1.la)
- Communicate their informed ideas, diverse perspectives, and creative solutions regarding energy issues through engaging in collaborative creative projects and thoughtful discussions. (NCCAS: Pr5.1.la, Re9.1.la)
- Engage respectfully in critical discussions about the multifaceted energy challenges facing the world and the various pathways toward sustainable and equitable energy solutions. (NCCAS: Re9.1.la, Cn11.1.la)
- Analyze and interpret the powerful ways in which the arts can be strategically used to raise public awareness, evoke emotional responses, and inspire meaningful action in relation to the urgent goals of Global Goal 7. (NCCAS: Re7.1.la, Re8.1.la)

### Attitudes
- Develop a heightened sense of empathy and concern for individuals and communities worldwide who currently lack access to affordable, reliable, and clean energy services, recognizing the inequities of energy poverty. (NCCAS: Re8.1.la, Cn10.1.la)
- Appreciate the fundamental importance of both individual and collective action in addressing complex energy challenges and transitioning towards a more sustainable energy future for all. (NCCAS: Cn11.1.la)
- Cultivate a strong sense of personal responsibility for their own energy consumption patterns and their direct and indirect impact on the environment and global sustainability efforts. (NCCAS: Cn11.1.la)
- Foster a positive and informed attitude towards the potential of renewable energy technologies and the adoption of sustainable practices as viable and essential solutions for a cleaner and more equitable energy future. (NCCAS: Cn10.1.la)

### Capacity
- Enhance their creativity, collaboration, and articulate communication skills through actively participating in creative arts activities centered around energy themes. (NCCAS: Cr2.1.la, Pr5.1.la)
- Develop advanced critical thinking skills to thoroughly analyze complex energy issues facing the world and to thoughtfully evaluate the feasibility and potential impact of various proposed sustainable solutions. (NCCAS: Re9.1.la, Cn10.1.la)
- Strengthen their ability to translate abstract scientific and social concepts related to energy into meaningful and impactful creative expressions across different artistic mediums. (NCCAS: Cr3.1.la)
- Gain increased confidence in using the arts as powerful tools for effective communication, persuasive advocacy, and inspiring positive action on critical global issues. (NCCAS: Pr6.1.la)

### Action
- Identify and evaluate concrete, age-appropriate actions they can take in their daily lives to conserve energy, promote energy efficiency, and support sustainable practices within their homes, schools, and communities. (NCCAS: Pr6.1.la, Cn11.1.la)
- Explore and develop meaningful ways to contribute to the achievement of Global Goal 7 at local, national, and even global levels through informed advocacy, raising awareness, and supporting relevant initiatives. (NCCAS: Cr3.1.la, Pr6.1.la)
- Consider and develop thoughtful strategies for how they can effectively use forms of creative expression to raise public awareness about pressing energy issues and inspire broader action towards a sustainable energy future for all. (NCCAS: Cr3.1.la, Pr6.1.la)
- Reflect critically on their individual and collective role in creating a more sustainable and equitable energy future for all, fostering a sense of sustained engagement and responsible global citizenship. (NCCAS: Re9.1.la, Cn11.1.la)

## Action Planning

Empowering youth to become active agents of change in the pursuit of ensuring access to affordable, reliable, sustainable, and modern energy for all. Recognizing that even small, consistent actions can contribute to a larger global impact.

### Small, Everyday Actions to Inspire Change
- Be Energy Conscious: Turn off lights, appliances, and electronics when not in use. Unplug chargers when devices are fully charged.
- Choose Energy-Efficient Options: Use LED light bulbs, energy-efficient appliances, and consider energy star ratings when purchasing new items.
- Reduce Consumption: Minimize your use of energy-intensive devices and activities. Consider biking, walking, or public transportation instead of driving.
- Conserve Water: Shorter showers and fixing leaks reduce the energy needed to heat and pump water.
- Advocate for Change: Talk to your family and friends about the importance of clean energy and sustainable practices.
- Stay Informed: Learn about energy issues and solutions, and share your knowledge with others.
- Reduce Waste: Recycling and reducing waste lowers the energy needed to produce new goods.
- Plant Trees: Trees absorb carbon dioxide and provide shade, reducing the need for air conditioning.
- Support Sustainable Businesses: Choose products and services from companies committed to environmental responsibility.

### Holistic Actions

#### Awareness and Advocacy (Using Your Voice)
- Organize presentations or discussions to raise awareness about Global Goal 7 and specific energy issues.
- Participate in campaigns advocating for policies that support clean energy and energy access.
- Use creative skills to create art, writing, or performances that highlight the importance of affordable and clean energy.

#### Local Engagement and Support (Acting in Your Community)
- Conduct energy audits in your school or community and develop action plans to improve energy efficiency.
- Volunteer with local organizations working on clean energy initiatives or energy assistance programs.
- Organize events to promote sustainable energy practices, such as workshops on energy conservation or renewable energy technologies.
- Advocate for clean energy policies at the local level, such as supporting community solar projects or energy-efficent building codes.

#### Global Citizenship and Learning (Expanding Your Understanding)
- Explore opportunities to connect with individuals in other countries to learn about their energy challenges and solutions.
- Organize screenings of documentaries or films that highlight global energy issues, followed by discussions.
- Research and share information about innovative energy technologies and policies from around the world.
- Support international organizations working to expand access to clean and affordable energy.
